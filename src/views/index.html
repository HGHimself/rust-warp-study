What do we do different?

Well the core driver of the network is exploration. The Digitheque is unlike a traditional social media network where the driver is interaction.

How do I use it?

The app is designed to be as easy as possible to use. There are two main concepts: the Link and the Page. A user can have many pages. A page can have at most 10 links. If you and another user have a page with the same link, you can see their page. That is it; no comments, no statuses, you will not even be able to see who owns that page.
